# `+++Brainstorm`

**Category:** Exploration & Planning

Generate multiple ideas without judging or filtering them.

**Structure:** `[Options] → [Optional Grouping] → [Next Steps]`

**Parameters:**
- `limit` (integer) — number of ideas to produce
- `diversity` (`low | medium | high`) — range and novelty of ideas

Postpone evaluation to `+++Critique` or `+++Refine`.
