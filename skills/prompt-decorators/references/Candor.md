# `+++Candor(level=LEVEL)`

**Category:** Evaluation & Refinement

Adjust the directness of feedback while remaining professional.

**Structure:** `[Candor Level] → [Direct Feedback] → [Rationale] → [Next Steps]`

**Parameter:** `level` — `low` (diplomatic), `medium` (balanced), `high` (blunt).
Respect the specified level and maintain an audience-appropriate tone throughout.
